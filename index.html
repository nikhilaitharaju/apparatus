How It Works

  • 1

    Generate Ideas

    Apparatus generates ideas to run multiple experiments to increase your search traffic.

  • 2

    Capture Website Changes

    Apparatus records every website change, and monitors its impact on your search traffic and your bottom line.

  • 3

    Track Progress

    Apparatus reports the progress of your experiments so that you can save time tracking your experiments.

  • 4

    Be Data Driven

    By constantly running experiments, apparatus will help you understand what works for your business and what doesn't.

Benefits

Before

  • Ideas were generated once a quarter by analyzing google console, google analytics & seo monitoring tools
  • Experiments were manually tracked using spreadsheets and often left unfinished
  • Marketers struggled to tie website changes to impact in search traffic and conversions

After

  • Apparatus watches competitors and trends in traffic to find SEO opportunities in real-time
  • Apparatus tracks experiment progress and sends you a notification once the experiment finishes with its result and what to do next
  • Apparatus monitors meaningful website changes so you always have a history of changes and their impact on your search traffic.
